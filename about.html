ABOUT

This is more or less a personal music blog. I put here the discoveries I have in music, audio production, music philosophy, music technology and the like. I write mostly about (but not limited to) modern progressive metal music and a little bit of jazz fusion. As a guitar musician, I want to discover more music that innovates and pushes the capabilities of the guitar.

Just a bit of a background, I attended music school so I have music theory fundamentals. I majored in guitar and minored in piano. Though I am not as adept in those instruments in a classical sense, I think I have enough knowledge to further my skills on my own. I was somewhat an average student. During my time in school, I had a huge interest in jazz fusion and improvisation so I took a separate, private lesson from my professor at the time.

Metal guitar, as less serious the term may be, to me has so much potential and appeal. In the digital age, there is an endless supply of content for every niche and progressive metal music is no exception. There are a lot of hidden artists and knowledge that I desire to uncover, which may be undervalued.

I intend to collectively put them here. This place will serve as a repository of the things I find important for guitar music. I also hope to evoke inspiration; encourage others to create and contribute to this beautiful art of progressive music.

May this message find you well.
